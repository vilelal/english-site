Santana de Parnaíba hosts Red Bull Bragantino x Internacional for the Brazilian Women's Championship

Publicado em: 26 de março de 2025

Santana de ParnaÃ-ba will be the stage for a great duel for the Brasileirão Feminino Série A1 this Wednesday (26). Red Bull Bragantino and Internacional face each other at 5 pm, in a match valid for the 2nd round of the competition, seeking to add important points to the table.

The Brasileirão Feminino Série A1 continues to gain prominence in the sports scene, and Santana de Parnaíba is consolidating itself as one of the stages of the sport, reinforcing the growth and appreciation of women's football in Brazil.
