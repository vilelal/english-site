With 8 swimming pools, Santana de Parnaíbba is a reference in water sports

Publicado em: 21 de março de 2025

The city of Parnaíba is a national reference when it comes to sports, especially water sports. Currently with eight pools, including in Alphaville, the city was a pioneer in introducing baby swimming in the metropolitan region of São Paulo, an innovative sport for children aged 6 months to 2 years and 11 months. With the aim of teaching babies to swim, this practice contributes to their sensory and motor development from the first months of life.

In addition to being an important sport, children's swimming and water activities promote health benefits for all ages, improving the functional capacity of the body and mind, improving physical performance and providing a healthier and more balanced lifestyle.

Santana de Parnaíba has 8 swimming pools


The municipality of Santana de Parnaíba offers a structure with eight sports units equipped with indoor and heated swimming pools in various neighborhoods of the city.

With the expansion of units, the city has stood out for providing more vacancies and modalities for the population.

Those interested in participating in swimming lessons should pay attention to vacancies, which can be found on the city hall website or on the parnaíba app.
